Do you want to start over the game?

  1. The first tile need to be at star icon to start the game.
  2. You need to make a valid English Word to get score.
  3. Each tile has a specific score at bottom right.
  4. If you draw space tile, you can pick any letter you want.
  5. You can earn double score of a letter if you place your tiles on "double letter scores".
  6. You can earn triple score of a letter if you place your tiles on "triple letter scores".
  7. You can earn double score of a word if you place your tiles on "double word scores".
  8. You can earn triple score of a word if you place your tiles on "triple word scores".
  9. When a tile place on the board, it cannot be moved.
  10. Do not allow a space between two letters of a word.
  11. You can earn +50 bonus point if you get a word with 7 letters.
  12. The game finish if there is no tiles remaining.
  13. Click "Save Word" to save your word when you done.
  14. You can use the letter of the saved words to make new words.
  15. Click "Reset Tiles" to call back letter on the board.
  16. Click "New Tiles" to get new tiles on the rack.
  17. Click "Start Over" to start the game again.
